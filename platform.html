Our Technology Layer

The infrastructure that makes
consistency possible.

Mana's proprietary technology layer sits beneath every clinic in our network. It standardises clinical protocols, enables data-driven personalisation, and captures outcomes across geographies — making every clinic more replicable and every franchise partner more effective.

What it does

Protocol standardisation at scale.

The central challenge in scaling any clinical service is maintaining quality as volume increases and geography diversifies. Our technology layer addresses this directly — not by simplifying clinical decisions, but by encoding expertise into a systematic framework that every clinician in our network can operate from.

Protocol Standardisation

Every clinical protocol deployed across our network is built to a common evidence standard and encoded within our technology layer. Clinicians operate from a defined, validated framework — not from ad-hoc judgment. This is what makes franchise-level consistency achievable.

Personalisation Framework

Standardised protocols are not one-size treatments. Our platform incorporates a data-informed personalisation layer that adapts protocol parameters to individual patient profiles, response patterns, and clinical history — improving outcomes while maintaining the consistency that makes measurement meaningful.

Outcomes Analytics

Every treatment session generates data. Our platform captures, structures, and analyses this data across our full clinical network — building an outcomes record that improves protocol accuracy over time, supports clinical governance, and generates the evidence base that underpins our investment case.

Franchise Enablement

Our platform makes the franchise model viable at scale. A new clinic partner — whether an independent neurologist, a multi-specialty hospital, or a co-branded operator — can plug into our technology layer and immediately operate to the same clinical standard as an established Anava location.

Clinical Governance

Our technology layer embeds clinical governance into the delivery process — not as an external audit function, but as an operational default. Session parameters, patient consent, contraindication screening, and protocol adherence are tracked systematically across every clinic in the network.

Geography-Agnostic Design

The platform is built to operate across diverse regulatory, clinical, and cultural environments. Whether a clinic is operating in Pune, Mumbai, or a European partner location, the technology layer adapts to local requirements while maintaining a common protocol standard at its core.

How it works

From platform to patient.

Our technology layer sits between Mana's clinical standards and each individual patient encounter — ensuring that what we know to be effective gets delivered consistently, regardless of which clinic or clinician is involved.

01

Protocol Development

Mana develops evidence-based protocols for each therapy modality and clinical indication, encoded in the platform layer.

02

Partner Onboarding

Franchise partners access the platform and receive training that aligns their clinical team with Mana's protocol standards.

03

Personalised Delivery

Each patient receives a protocol adapted to their individual profile — standardised in structure, personalised in parameters.

04

Outcomes Capture

Session data flows back to the platform, improving protocol accuracy and building the network-wide evidence base over time.
