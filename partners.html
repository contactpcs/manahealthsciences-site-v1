Franchise & Partners

You bring clinical expertise.
We bring the rest.

Mana Health Sciences partners with independent neurologists, psychiatrists, and multi-specialty hospital systems to deliver validated neuromodulation therapies under a standardised clinical framework. You focus on patients. We provide protocol, platform, and brand.

The proposition

What you get when you partner with Mana.

Entering the neuromodulation space independently requires significant clinical, operational, and commercial infrastructure. Partnering with Mana gives you access to all of it from day one.

Clinical Protocol Framework

Our evidence-based protocol library covers tDCS, TMS, and TPS across multiple indications. You don't build protocols from scratch — you operate from a validated, structured framework developed by our clinical team and refined by network-wide outcomes data.

Platform Access

Partner clinics connect to the Mana technology layer — receiving access to our protocol delivery system, patient management tools, and outcomes tracking infrastructure. This is the same platform powering the Anava network.

Training & Onboarding

We provide structured clinical training for your team, covering protocol delivery, patient assessment, contraindication screening, and outcomes measurement. Onboarding is designed to get your clinic to protocol-standard quickly, without a lengthy ramp period.

Brand & Co-branding

Partner clinics can operate under the Anava brand, as a co-branded facility (e.g. "[Hospital] powered by Anava"), or — for qualified hospital systems — under a white-label arrangement. Each model gives you access to the clinical and commercial value of the Mana platform under the right brand structure for your context.

Ongoing Clinical Support

Partnership doesn't end at onboarding. Our clinical team provides ongoing protocol support, case-level guidance when needed, and regular updates to the protocol library as the evidence base evolves. You are supported, not left to operate in isolation.

Referral Network

As part of the Mana network, you benefit from inter-clinic referral opportunities and the broader network's clinical reputation. In markets where multiple network clinics operate, we coordinate referrals and ensure patients reach the right facility for their needs.

Who we partner with

Two types of partner. One platform.

Independent Clinicians

Neurologists, psychiatrists, and rehabilitation physicians looking to add neuromodulation services to their existing practice. We provide the clinical framework, equipment guidance, and platform access to make this viable without building from scratch.

  • Operate under the Anava brand or co-branded
  • Protocol and training provided
  • Suitable for single-clinic or multi-clinic operators
Enquire as a Clinician

Hospital Systems

Multi-specialty hospitals and healthcare groups looking to establish or expand a neuromodulation department. We offer co-branding and white-label arrangements for institutional partners — giving your hospital access to our platform, protocols, and clinical governance framework under your own brand or jointly with Anava.

  • Co-brand or white-label options available
  • Full platform and governance framework
  • Suitable for multi-site rollouts
