Clinical Network

Where the platform
meets the patient.

Anava Clinics is Mana's patient-facing clinical brand — the network of clinics operating on our platform and delivering tDCS, TMS, and TPS therapies under clinical supervision. Anava is not a separate company. It is the clinical expression of the Mana platform in action.

The relationship

Mana sets the standard.
Anava delivers it.

Mana Health Sciences owns the technology layer, develops the clinical protocols, and sets the operational standards that govern every clinic in our network. Anava Clinics is the patient-facing brand that delivers those standards in practice — to real patients, in real clinical settings, in India.

This architecture is deliberate. Separating the corporate platform brand from the patient-facing clinical brand gives us the ability to scale the Anava network through franchising, co-branding, and white-label partnerships without diluting either identity.

Every Anava clinic, whether directly operated or operated by a franchise partner, runs on Mana's platform — and every session generates data that flows back to the platform, making the whole network smarter over time.

For investors

Why the network matters to the investment thesis.

Anava Clinics is not simply a clinical operation. It is the proof-of-concept and evidence base for the Mana platform — demonstrating in live clinical settings that our protocol infrastructure delivers consistent, measurable outcomes at scale.

Live outcomes data

Every Anava clinic session generates real-world outcomes data. This is a growing, proprietary evidence base that strengthens the investment case over time — not hypothetical projections, but clinical records.

Franchise-ready proof point

An operational Anava clinic demonstrates that the Mana platform is deployable in a real clinical environment. Each additional clinic location validates the franchise model and demonstrates replicability to prospective partners and investors.

India as foundation market

India is not a secondary market for Mana — it is the primary operational base. A large, fast-growing economy with significant unmet demand in neurological and psychiatric care, operated by a team with direct local expertise.
