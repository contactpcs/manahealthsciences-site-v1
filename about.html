About Mana Health Sciences

Built on a belief that
access is the missing variable.

Most healthcare markets have the demand. Most have the clinical talent. What they lack is the infrastructure to distribute quality consistently and at scale. That is what Mana was founded to build.

मन

Our founding

The problem was never
the capability.

Across specialised healthcare, the same pattern repeats. The clinical knowledge exists. The practitioners exist. The patient demand is real. What is absent is the infrastructure — the standardised, scalable systems that allow quality to be reproduced consistently, across geographies and across operators.

Mana Health Sciences was founded to be that infrastructure layer. We build the platform — the technology, the operational standards, the franchise architecture, the regulatory and compliance frameworks — and we deploy it through a portfolio of clinical sub-holdings and licensed partners.

Our first operational holding, Anava Clinics, is live in India. It is proof of the model. And it is the first of several brands that will run on the Mana platform as we grow.

Mission

Build the platform infrastructure that makes specialised healthcare consistently deliverable, at scale, across the world's most underserved markets.

We exist to own and operate the shared layer — technology, standards, franchise systems, regulatory frameworks — that allows clinical brands to scale without reinventing the wheel in every new market they enter.

Vision

A world where geography does not determine access to the highest standard of specialised care.

In the near term, that means India and Europe. In the longer term, it means any market where the gap between what is clinically possible and what patients can consistently access remains wider than it should be.
